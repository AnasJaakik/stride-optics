Camera Angle

Video Quality

  • Duration: 10-20 seconds of continuous running

Subject Positioning

  • Visibility: Both legs fully visible throughout

Lighting & Background

  • Lighting: Even, bright, no harsh shadows

Running Conditions

  • Surface: Flat, level (treadmill or track)

Common Issues to Avoid

  • Camera too close or too far
  • Angled view (not true rear view)
  • Poor lighting or shadows on legs
  • Motion blur or camera shake
  • Legs crossing or occluded
  • Video too short (< 5 seconds)
